03 · And then fixes

Every finding becomes a move.

Detection that doesn't change anything is only a report. In stardust, each tension is paired with the specific redesign decision that resolves it.

Detected
Ad-hoc type scale. Headings at 48 → 44 → 40 → 32px sit so close they all shout at the same volume. No consistent ratio: the page-builder fingerprint.
→
Stardust uplift
Impose a modular scale. A single perfect-fourth (~1.25) ramp gives the hero a dominant size and restores typographic rhythm and depth.
Detected
Fragmented CTA voice. Three sign-up labels and three "see more" variants: 21 distinct CTA labels with no canonical action. The eye doesn't know where to go.
→
Stardust uplift
One canonical primary. Collapse to a single hot-pink primary action with a quiet secondary text-link; one signal, repeated.
Detected
The brand under-shows itself. The signature magenta covers a fraction of the screen; the best asset, an interactive feature switcher, is buried mid-page at small scale.
→
Stardust uplift
Amplify what's ownable. Promote the live feature switcher, foreground the magenta→coral gradient, and let the product build itself on screen.
