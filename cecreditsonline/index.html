02 — What's worth resolving

Tensions identified

Five specific observations from the captured surface — the load-bearing input for the redesign.

dated-pattern

Stock-Shopify hero

A single scrimmed photo behind a centered white headline + green button — indistinguishable from a thousand theme demos. A 25-year accredited provider's hero should carry authority and route teachers to their credit path.

ia-clutter

The conversion fork looks like a footnote

"Get the Credits You Need" — the chooser that routes a teacher to the currency their district accepts (NYC P/A+, LAUSD points, Chicago lane credits, Texas CPE, WA clock hours) — is a flat row of 14px icon-links buried mid-page.

contrast-density

Soft, undifferentiated hierarchy

One green carries every emphasis with no tonal ladder, and the heading scale is ad-hoc (40→35→30.8→25…) across 3+ families (Mundial, Figtree, Poppins, stray Inter). Nothing leads the eye.

cliché

A discount popup over the proof

A "Summer Sale — 15% off" modal overlays the hero on load, pushing an accredited, trust-led brand toward discount-store tone — and burying the value proposition behind a coupon.

missed-opportunity

The two strongest assets are scattered, not orchestrated

Genuine classroom photography is cropped to thumbnail tiles, and the proof points (19,047 verified evaluations, 94% of 20,000+ educators, university accreditation logos) are sprinkled across separate sections. Together they could be the page's credibility backbone; today they read as incidental.

03 — Three directions

The variants

All three are brand-faithful: the captured cream/green palette, Mundial + Figtree + Poppins, real classroom photography, and the real proof numbers. They differ by axis — A fixes the shape, B bets on IA, C bets on motion + photographic scale.

ARisk-averse green-light

Tomorrow's version of the site you have today

"Yes, that's us — fixed."

The captured IA kept intact, with the five tensions resolved.

  • Replace the stock-Shopify scrimmed hero with an institutionally confident one
  • Lift the "Get the Credits You Need" chooser into a scannable, weighted selector
  • Tighten the type system — one modular scale, drop stray Inter
  • Remove the discount modal from the load-bearing hero
  • Orchestrate the scattered proof into a coherent trust spine; give photography room

05 — Notes

What's real vs. placeholder

The logo, the genuine classroom photography, the university accreditation context and the real proof numbers (19,047 verified evaluations; 94% of 20,000+ educators; accredited since 2001) are captured from the live site and reused verbatim. Any other figure is rendered as [data-placeholder] and should be confirmed before anything ships. The report chrome substitutes Figtree for the commercial Mundial display face, which isn't web-available; the prototypes use the captured font where present.

Validation: all three render with zero console errors; Mode A pinned. Variant C additionally passes motion validation — every animation neutralizes to the static reference under reduced-motion.
