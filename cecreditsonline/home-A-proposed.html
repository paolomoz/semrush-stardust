About

What is CE Credits Online?

CE Credits Online, a dba of The Riesling Group, Inc., has been creating and providing graduate-level, professional development courses for K-12 educators since 2001. For over 15 years we have offered professional development courses that have an immediate impact in the classroom.

We create timely, relevant and research-based courses — from 21st-century teaching practices to classroom management and social-emotional learning — that teachers can apply the very next day.
